Caramel Popcorn

Description

Tasty sweet popcorn perfect for those with a sweet tooth!
This recipe is simple, but will take some time as you wait for your popcorn to pop!
This perfect for family and friends who also enjoy a sugary treat!
Warning: This recipe will involve trying to mix hot and boiling caramel so beware!

ingredients

  • Two cups of Popcorn kernals
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. 1.Pour two cups of popcorn kernals in pot
  2. 2.Pour four tablespoon of oil
  3. 3.Turn the heat to high for the stove plate and wait for the popcorn to pop
  4. 4.Place the popped popcorn in a glass bowl
  5. 5.In another pot, scoop 3 tablespoons of butter into the pot
  6. 6.Once the butter has been melted, add in 2 cups of sugar
