Scrambled Eggs

Description

This is a recipe on how to make scramnled eggs!
Scrambeled eggs are simple and a good source of protein. It only requires 5 ingredients. The eggs don't take that long to make so you will have breakfast right away in the morning.

ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt (Optional)
  • Oil (Optional)

Steps

  1. 1. Pour 1 teaspoon of oil into a pan
  2. 2. Add in a quarter of a teaspoon of a butter
  3. 3. Crack the two large eggs into a bowl
  4. 4. Pour in milk into the bowl
  5. 5. Once the butter has melted pour in the eggs
  6. 6. Mix the eggs continuously
  7. 7. Move the pan off the stove plate for 30 seconds while mixing the eggs
  8. 8. Move the pan back on the stove palte while mixing for a 1 minute and repeat for 6 minutes
  9. 9. You can serve you eggs on a plate/bowl
  10. 10. Enjoy!
